CafePal Ordering Service

CafePal is a creative tech startup ordering service that is geared towards independent coffee shops who have a difficult time competing against large coffee chains in the city. They also suffer from a reputation for providing poor quality of food and not as organized as the coffee franchise. CafePal Company makes it easier than ever for independent coffee shops to improve their reputation and become efficient in various types of business processes such as payments, personnel, and marketing. To use the app, simply download the app (iOS & Android), view the available registered cafes you want to visit, and CafePal will take care of payment methods an convenience. You can even manage your favourite cafes around the city and explore more cafes around your neighbourhood. For all registered independent coffee shop owners, we provide analytics on what products consumers like the most.

Shinmunji Website

Shinmunji is the email newsletter for the individuals who want to become more knowledgeable about what is currently going on in South Korea. Shinmunji team collects the most trendy topic and turn it into witty and knowledgeable article that comes through your email inbox every morning, 6:00 AM. It allows the person to start the day with the latest news that can turn into an effective conversation starter. Subscribers can refer, advertise, and collect Shinmunji goods if they refer Shinmunji to their friends. Through email based technology, subscribers can easily access newsletters on a daily basis during commutes and so on.

ArmyPal Subscription Service

ArmyPal is a subscription service designed to help you create monthly subscription box for loved one who are serving army duty. Users can create profiles and fill out information about what the person would enjoy receving while serving in the army. Using the picker technology, a user can request assistance and provide information about their occupations and so on. After a task is complete, ArmyPal's Artificial Intelligence software will review the results and recommend appropriate items that will be included in the box.

The app is designed to promote maintenance of social bonds between people and ones who are serving the army duty.
